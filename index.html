üß† Why Grounding Matters (10 minutes)

Your Nervous System 101

Click on each zone to explore how your nervous system works:

üü¢ Green Zone

Calm & Safe

What it feels like: Thinking clearly, feeling connected to yourself and others, able to make good decisions

Body signs: Relaxed breathing, comfortable muscle tension, present and alert

This is your optimal state for learning and connecting!

üü° Yellow Zone

Fight or Flight

What it feels like: Anxious, angry, restless, can't sit still, mind racing

Body signs: Fast heartbeat, shallow breathing, tense muscles, fidgety

Your body is trying to protect you by preparing for action

üî¥ Red Zone

Shutdown

What it feels like: Numb, spacey, can't think or feel clearly, disconnected

Body signs: Low energy, feeling heavy, hard to move or speak

Your body is protecting you by slowing everything down

üåü How Movement Helps

  • Gets you back to Green Zone: Movement helps regulate your nervous system naturally
  • Helps you notice: Builds awareness of what zone you're in right now
  • Gives your body a voice: Movement is your body's language for feelings
  • Creates safety from the inside out: Your body learns it can handle different states
